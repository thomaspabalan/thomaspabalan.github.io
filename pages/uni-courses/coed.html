  • Bachelor of Secondary Education major in Social Studies
  • Bachelor of Secondary Education major in Sciences

The College of Teacher Education, with its promise to deliver quality and globally competitive instruction, seeks to meet the operational criteria and service standards as evaluated by the Accrediting Agency of Chartered Colleges and Universities in the Philippines (AACCUP) Inc.


As the College of Education continues to evolve and grow, it remains dedicated to its mission of transforming the landscape of teaching. Through its commitment to excellence, innovation, and the introduction of new programs, the COED strives to be at the forefront of educational instruction and to produce highly skilled and competent educators who will shape the future of the teaching profession.


In its end, the College of Education hopes to continue the journey of transforming the landscape of teaching and become a champion of creativity and innovation in Teacher Education.
