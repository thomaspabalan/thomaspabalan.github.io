CET COURSES

Currently, the College of Engineering and Technology offers the following course/s:


  • Bachelor of Science in Civil Engineering major in Structural Engineering
  • Bachelor of Science in Electrical Engineering
  • Bachelor of Science in Mechanical Engineering
  • Bachelor of Science in Industrial Engineering
  • Bachelor of Science in Electronics Engineering
  • Bachelor of Industrial Technology major in Automotive Technology
  • Bachelor of Science in Industrial Technology major in Mechatronics Technology

  • Master of Science in Civil Engineering major in Structural Engineering
  • Master of Science in Electrical Engineering major in Power System

The College of Engineering and Technology aims to develop and produce professional engineers and technologist imbued with desirable work ethics, leadership and entrepreneurial capability with the necessary knowledge and skills for them to become globally competitive and productive citizens of the country.
