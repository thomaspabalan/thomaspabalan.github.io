COS COURSES

Currently, the College of Science offers the following course/s:


  • Bachelor of Science in Nursing
  • Bachelor of Science in Chemistry
  • Bachelor of Science in Food Technology
  • Bachelor of Science in Mathematics
  • Bachelor of Science in Environmental Science

  • Master of Arts in Nursing with specialization in Nursing Service Administration (Thesis)

The College of Science commits to develop critical thinking, polish minds, nurture values and attain excellence in instruction, research, and extension. To date, the College of Science continues to fulfill its mandate to produce quality graduates in the different fields of Science and Technology who are equipped with the knowledge, technical skills, and ethical values needed for people empowerment through instruction, research and extension services.
