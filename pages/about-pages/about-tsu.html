History of Thomas State University

In 1906, a visionary by the name of T.C. Pabalan embarked on an extraordinary educational journey that would forever change the landscape of higher learning. Pabalan's dream was to establish a place where practical skills and knowledge would converge, thus giving birth to what would become Thomas State University.


Early Years as a Trade School (1906-1950s)

In its formative years, Thomas State University served as a renowned trade school. It was a place where eager learners flocked to acquire essential vocational skills, from carpentry to metalworking. T.C. Pabalan's commitment to bridging the gap between traditional academia and the real world was evident. The institution quickly became an indispensable part of the local community, providing individuals with the practical expertise needed to thrive in their chosen fields.


Transition to Academic Degrees (Early 1960s)

The early 1960s marked a transformative period in the history of Thomas State University. Driven by a commitment to offer more comprehensive educational opportunities, the university expanded its offerings to include academic degrees. This pivotal shift opened the doors to a broader range of disciplines, from liberal arts and sciences to engineering and business.


State University Status (1989)

In 1989, Thomas State University achieved a significant milestone when it was granted State University status. This recognition was a testament to the institution's dedication to providing quality education and its pivotal role in fostering local and regional development. The university's commitment to its community and the pursuit of academic excellence had reached a new pinnacle.


Continued Growth and Excellence (1990s-Present)

Since attaining State University status, Thomas State University has continually expanded its academic offerings, campus facilities, and research capabilities. The university has emerged as a hub of innovation and discovery, where students, faculty, and staff collaborate to address some of the world's most pressing challenges.


Today, Thomas State University is rapidly making a name for itself as a center of excellence in education, research, and innovation. Its journey, rooted in the vision of T.C. Pabalan, reflects the spirit of dedication and the pursuit of knowledge that continues to inspire generations of students, faculty, and alumni. As the university looks ahead to the future, it remains committed to the principles of excellence and inclusivity, with a profound dedication to shaping a better world through education and research.
