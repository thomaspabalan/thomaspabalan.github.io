CCS contender Thomas Ciubal bags Mr. TSU 2nd Runner Up

Following a challenging competition against fellow students aspiring for the title of the next king, Thomas Ciubal, a delegate from the College of Computer Studies, achieved the remarkable distinction of 2nd Runner-up at the recently concluded Mr. and Ms. TSU 2023 on October 18th, held at TSU Gymnasium.


Ciubal's path to success was guided by a set of values instilled in him by the dedicated educators at our university. He expressed deep gratitude for their tireless efforts, particularly on the occasion of #WorldTeachersDay, during the QnA segment. It was with unwavering determination, boundless passion, unyielding commitment, and sheer perseverance that Ciubal excelled during the event.


"Determination is my driving force; I never surrender. Passion fuels my every endeavor, as I consistently give my best. Perseverance is my strength; no matter the challenges, I persist, push through, and continue the fight," he emphasized when asked to describe the value that best characterizes his role as a dedicated student of our university.


Ciubal also received the prestigious Best in Talent Award for his captivating shadow play performance, which was based on Freddie Aguilar's renowned song "Anak." He aims to serve as a role model and an inspiration for fellow students, encouraging them to emulate his journey and achievements.
