Thomas Chester Pabalan emerges victorious as 1st Place winner in SISS - CCS Week Quiz Game

The TSU CCS Society for Information Systems Students (SISS) organized a Quiz Game in celebration of 'CCS Week,' engaging CCS Red Hawks in a thrilling intellectual challenge. Students from various year levels were pitted against a range of questions, ranging from the simple to the complex, in a quest for the top positions. The SISS proudly recognized the five victors with the highest scores in the competition.


Thomas Chester C. Pabalan secured the coveted 1st place, while Bea S. Arias claimed 2nd place. Arvin Errol E. Liscano earned the distinction of 3rd place, while Clarence R. Deseo and Cristel Lumba walked away with the 4th and 5th prizes, respectively.


The SISS extended their heartfelt appreciation to all the participants, and they wholeheartedly congratulated the winners for their impressive performance.
