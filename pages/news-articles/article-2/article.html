What is the News Section About?

The news section of the website shows current and past information about events that relate to the University such as the conduction of University events and holidays, University competitions, and general University-wide announcements. It consists of two parts: the preview, which is found in the news section, and the article itself.


The HTML and CSS of both the preview and the main article have been designed with flexibility in mind. Previews and articles may not have thumbnails or pictures attached to them (like this one) and article content previews can also be shown (like this one again) or not shown (all other article previews) depending on the number of elements currently being used in the preview. The article container automatically resizes based on how much content is present in the article.
